About Meta Particle

Meta Particle is a massively multiplayer online 3D space shooter,
where you pilot your ship in a large space populated by
stars, planets and other spacey stuff.

You may play as one of three/four races, each with its own agenda.
You may also join one of the many factions ingame and work together
to bring down another faction, or why not another race?
